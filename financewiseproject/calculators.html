Financial Calculators

Four simple beginner-friendly calculators to help you plan
your loans, interest, and budget splits.

Choose a Calculator

Enter your values and click the Calculate button to see instant results.

1. EMI Calculator

EMI = Equated Monthly Installment. This is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan.
Formula: EMI = [P × r × (1+r)n] / [(1+r)n – 1]

2. Simple Interest Calculator

Simple Interest is calculated only on the original principal amount.
Formula: SI = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

3. Compound Interest Calculator

Compound Interest is calculated on the principal AND on the interest earned so far.
Formula: A = P × (1 + r/n)nt, CI = A – P

4. 50-30-20 Budget Calculator

The 50-30-20 rule divides your monthly income into three categories: 50% Needs, 30% Wants, 20% Savings.

Formula Reference Table

Quick reference for all formulas used in the calculators above.

# Calculator Formula Variables
1 EMI EMI = [P × r × (1+r)n] / [(1+r)n – 1] P=Principal, r=Monthly Rate, n=Months
2 Simple Interest SI = (P × R × T) / 100 P=Principal, R=Rate, T=Time (years)
3 Compound Interest A = P × (1 + r/n)nt P=Principal, r=Rate, n=Frequency, t=Time
4 50-30-20 Budget Needs=50%, Wants=30%, Savings=20% Based on monthly net income
